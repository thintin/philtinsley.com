About Me

25+ years of doing the work — not just advising on it.

My Background

I've spent over 25 years working in social care and housing, starting as a support worker and progressing through every level — team leader, manager, operations director — before setting up my own care business, Step Forward Support CIC, which I still run today.

That journey means I understand what it's like on the front line, in the boardroom, and everywhere in between. When I talk about operational change, I'm drawing on direct experience of implementing it — not just theorising about it.

Why Consultancy?

Running a care business through the last decade has meant navigating constant change: tightening regulations, workforce challenges, digital transformation, and now the AI revolution. I've had to figure out what works in practice, not just in principle.

Along the way, I kept being asked by other business owners and leadership teams for advice — on systems, processes, compliance, technology adoption, and managing change without losing what makes their organisation effective. That's what led me to formalise this work as a consultancy.

What I Bring

I'm not a theoretical consultant. I don't hand over a report and leave. My approach is hands-on, practical, and grounded in the reality of how organisations actually work.

Practitioner first

I still run a care business. Every recommendation I make is tested against the reality of running an actual organisation with real staff, real service users, and real budgets.

Every level of experience

Support worker through to director. I understand the concerns at every level when change is introduced — and how to address them.

Technology pragmatist

I'm excited about what AI and digital tools can do, but only when they serve the business. I'll tell you what's worth investing in and what's hype.

Implementation focus

Strategy is important, but it's worthless without execution. I stay involved through implementation to make sure changes actually stick.

Sectors & Experience

My core expertise is in adult social care — supported living, domiciliary care, residential care, and housing. But the operational challenges I help with are universal: process inefficiency, technology adoption, quality systems, and managing people through change.

I also work with SMEs outside of care who are facing similar challenges — particularly around digital transformation and AI adoption.
